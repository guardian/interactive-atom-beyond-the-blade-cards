Beyond the Blade

We tracked all the knife deaths of children and teens in Great Britain in 2017 and explored the themes that emerged.
Gary Younge accepted Feature Writer of the year at the UK Press Awards for the team. Judges described the series as: “brilliant in-depth journalism that told a familiar story in a new way [and] had a real impact”
